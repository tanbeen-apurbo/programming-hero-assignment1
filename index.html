Got Questions?

Everything you need to know before you register.

Is there a refund policy if I can't attend?

Cancellations made before August 15, 2026 receive a full refund. Between August 16 and September 1, you get a 50% refund or a free ticket transfer. No refunds after September 1, but transfers are always free.

Can I attend remotely if I can't travel?

DevConf 2026 is an in-person event. Pro and Team tickets include lifetime access to session recordings. Standard ticket holders can add recording access at checkout.

Do you offer discounts for students or groups?

Students get 40% off the Standard ticket with a valid .edu email, applied automatically at checkout. Groups of 5 or more should contact us about the Team plan for volume pricing.

What's included in the conference badge?

Every ticket includes keynotes, breakout sessions, lunch, coffee breaks, and evening networking events. Pro and Team badges also include workshops, the VIP dinner, and speaker meet and greets.

Where can I find visa invitation letters?

After you register, an invitation letter is available in your attendee dashboard. Contact support if you need it faster for a visa appointment.
